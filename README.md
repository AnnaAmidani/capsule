# Capsule

A personal space for memories, sealed and delivered when the time is right.

---

## What it is

Capsule is a web platform for creating time capsules. You gather what matters - words, images, links, keepsakes - lock it away, and choose when it arrives. It might be a message to your children, a note to a future version of yourself, or a shared memory from a wedding that opens on the couple's tenth anniversary.

The two states of a capsule are the heart of the product: **sealed** (the contents are locked and inaccessible, even to you) and **opened** (delivered to your recipients at the moment you chose). The architecture enforces this, not just the interface.

---

## Who it's for

**Legacy.** Parents writing to children not yet old enough to understand. People facing illness. Anyone who wants to leave something behind that arrives at the right time.

**Celebration.** A love letter that opens on a future anniversary. A birthday message from someone who won't be there. A graduation note sealed when someone was still in their first year.

**Community memory.** A group captures a shared moment - a trip, a wedding, a milestone - and seals it to be opened years later, together.

---

## What goes in a capsule

- Written notes and letters
- Photos and images
- YouTube video links
- Spotify and YouTube playlist links
- Event keepsakes (screenshots of tickets and programs)

Music and external video are link-based. Capsule does not store audio files.

---

## How it works

1. **Create** a capsule and add whatever you want to preserve.
2. **Seal** it. Once sealed, the contents are locked. No edits, no previews.
3. **Set a delivery date** and add the email addresses of your recipients.
4. **It arrives.** On the chosen date, recipients get an email with a link to open the capsule.

## Tiers

| Tier | Storage | Capsules | Sharing |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| Seed (free) | 5 GB | 1 | Public only |
| Vessel | 25 GB | Unlimited | Public and private |
| Legacy | 100 GB+ | Unlimited | Public and private, plus digital executor |

Monthly and yearly billing. Private capsules (visible only to designated recipients) require Vessel or above.

## MVP scope

**What's included in the first release:**

- Account creation and login (email/password and Google/Apple)
- Capsule creation with text, images, and links
- Scheduled delivery by date
- Recipient designation by email
- Sealed and opened capsule states
- Email delivery notification
- Free tier and one paid tier

**Deliberately out of scope for now:**

- Trigger-based delivery (life events, trusted contacts)
- 3D capsule visualization
- Digital executor system
- Zero-knowledge encryption
- Community and social features
